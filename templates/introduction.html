INTRODUCTION

Blockbuster - Movie Analysis Project

What if you could predict the next big blockbuster?

The movie industry is one of the most profitable industries worldwide. Billions of dollars go into the box office yearly. Individual movies can also make over one billion dollars. We decided to explore what makes a movie popular. What can a movie do in order to make these billions of dollars? In order to do this, we analyzed a dataset called "IMDB 5000 Movie Dataset" from data.world. This dataset over 5000 movie titles spanning across 100 years and 66 countries, with 28 distinct variables, 2399 director names and additional information on the main actors/actresses.
